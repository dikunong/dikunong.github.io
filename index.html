About me

I'm a Software Engineering student in my 2nd year of studies at the University of Auckland. I've been working with technology since my childhood, but who hasn't these days? More recently I started programming, and now I'm seeking internship opportunities in New Zealand and overseas. I'm relatively new to the world of software, but working hard everyday to learn new skills and try new things, and would love to bring my drive and dedication to the workplace.

In my spare time I like to light, write, and break the rule of threes by not giving a third word that rhymes. More specifically, I've done theatrical lighting design for the Auckland Engineering Revue and my high school; I also write various things when the opportunity arises, usually with the intent of making people laugh. I just started a stint as the University of Auckland Engineering Part II Student Blogger, so over the next 5 months I'll be posting regularly about life as a Part II Software student!
